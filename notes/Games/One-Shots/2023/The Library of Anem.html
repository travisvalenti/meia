The Library of Anēm

Not Done
Adventure
Last updated on July 4, 2023

Pronunciation: An-eem

Please note that this adventure has not been fully planned at this point. You will be notified if any of the below expectations change.

Expected Gameplay

Roleplay5/5
Combat5/5
Lore5/5
Length3-5 Sessions
Level10 and 11 (maybe 9, unsure at this time)
AncestriesAny (including Goblin)
ClassesAny (recommend at least 1 occultism expert)

Overview

The Leviathans have awoken and are wreaking havoc all over the civilized world. The Tarrasque has proven indestructable, and the Kraken has yet to lose a battle; A mysterious floating creature has emerged from Lake Siyah and a gargantuan sand-worm has started attacking travellers in the Dry Sea.

You and your allies have volunteered or been elected to try and find a way to stop it. The Manan Viscount Incarceration claims that these creatures were detained for millenia by an ancient ritual known as ‘The Pact of Slumber’, but that it has recently been broken.

Find out the location of the ancient and mysterious Library of Anēm, then venture inside to find the lost knowledge of the Pact of Slumber.

This adventure ends when either the Pact of Slumber ritual is recovered, or all party members have died (or both).

Advisories

  • This adventure includes memory loss effects, which may result in certain characters irreversably (except for divine intervention) forgetting large portions of memory, at the end of the adventure.
  • This adventure ends with an “Extreme” rating encounter difficulty, characters may die.
  • This adventure is moderately lore-heavy.

Character requirements

  • Each character must have a reason to stay with the party (though they do not need to be lawful or good), and a reason to want to finish the quest (this can be because they don’t want the world to end).
  • All characters require a country of origin. Recommendations are The Kingdom of Mana, The Iron Empire, Qalb Alsahra or Balahel/Lake Siyah.
  • Characters will begin at level 10 (maybe lower) and should be prepared to become level 11 toward the end of the adventure.

Starting gold and items

Starting gold and items will be finalized at a later date, but expect to have at least the following, per-player:

  • 2 permanent 11th level items
  • 2 permanent 10th level items
  • 2 consumable 11th level items
  • 2 consumable 10th level items
  • 2 consumable 9th level items
  • 500 gp
