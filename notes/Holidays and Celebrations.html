Hearthday - 15th of Nights (01-15)

Hearthday is a day to rest and spend time with loved ones. It is the Material plane’s equivalent of Christmas and Thanksgiving.

New Year’s Day - 1st of Frost (01-01)

New Year’s Day is the first day of the year, and a time for many to make plans and set goals for the year ahead.

Festival of Flowers - Last of Flower (05-30)

The Festival of Flowers is held on the last of Flower each year, and commemorates the end of spring and the beginning of summer. Any flowers that are blooming on this day are woven into intricate artworks and placed in town squares.
